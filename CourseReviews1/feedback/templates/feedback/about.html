About AUC Course Review

This platform is an anonymous course review website dedicated to the students of the Amsterdam University College (AUC). It is developed by students with the intention of sharing unbiased course experiences to guide fellow peers in their academic journeys.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of shared knowledge and experiences to help our student community make informed decisions about their courses. Usernames are not displayed to maintain anonymity and encourage openness.

Review Guidelines

While we encourage freedom of speech and honest feedback, we also stress the importance of respect and constructiveness in your reviews. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Do not post hateful or discriminatory comments.
  • Be honest but also considerate; your review should aim to guide and help other students, not to harm.
  • Remember that your review could have a significant impact on someone's decisions, so be as factual and clear as possible.

Profanity Filter

To maintain the professionalism and courtesy of our platform, we use the Profanity Python library to filter out any offensive language from the reviews.

Disclaimer

The views and opinions expressed in the reviews are those of individual users and do not necessarily reflect the official policy or position of the developers of this platform or AUC.
